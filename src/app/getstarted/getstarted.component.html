Welcome to Experizer

What is Experizer?

Experizer is an online VR Content creation software that requires no programming. You can create immersive VR experiences by customising through a range of VR Experience templates that we have created for you and shall keep innovating more over our forthcoming upgrades. We have kept the idea of a ‘Subject Matter Expert’ customising an Experizer Virtual Experience with just simple drag and drops and content like images, videos, audios or simply text.

Experizer is available as a Software-as-a-Service. You don’t require any installation on your machine to Author a Virtual Experience and hence anyone having access to internet can practically experience the power of customising a Virtual Experience.

The power of Experizer.

Experizer can help you stitch awesome Virtual Experience’s in matter of minutes. Be that a Virtual Tour or a Movie Theater, you can have that up an running in matter of minutes (if you have the content with you) instead of those long months that you spend with your developers. Also we have ensured that the entire process of customisation does not require any programming knowledge. Simply drag and drop, click and customise with your content. There are options to create Assessments that can sync up with your existing learning infrastructure provided they support xAPI standard. The best part is the compatibility.

The product output is compatible with almost all popular VR glasses including

  • Google Cardboard
  • Google Daydream
  • Samsung GearVR
  • Oculus Go
  • Oculus Rift.
Who can use Experizer?

Anyone who wants to leverage on the power of Virtual Reality and wants to create a Virtual Experience can use Experizer.

  Training and Development professionals who wish to create Virtual Reality experiences for teaching and assessing the knowledge of a learner.
 University and Colleges who wish to teach through Immersive learning techniques.
 Corporates who wish to give a Virtual Tour of their facility to their potential clients or employees or create engagements through the power of Virtual Reality.
 Real Estate Agencies who wish to create a Virtual Tour of their property.
