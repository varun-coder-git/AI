How to manage your account and its features.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use Experizer?

Absolutely not! Anybody who is familiar with using a document editor can use Experizer and create highly interactive and immersive VR experiences within a matter of few minutes.

How can I make the VR experiences available to my Patrons?

Patrons can access your VR experiences either by

  • Either directly browsing the link you can share with them on their Mobile or Desktop browsers
  • Downloading the Experizer app on their device(if supported)
Will my Patrons need specific app/ VR goggles to view the VR experiences?

Thought for best experience we recommend using VR goggle’s however your Patrons can also browse the VR experience directly using their Mobile/Desktop browsers.

Can I try it before I decide to subscribe for it?

Of course. Just sign up for a 14-day fully-functional trial here.

What are the restrictions of the trial version?

The trial is a fully-functional trial. You can try all the features of the product. There are some restrictions on the available storage space and number of views.

Do I need any specific machine configuration to use Experizer?

Nothing specific – Experizer works on all the standard WIndows, Linux and Mac machines.

Will the VR experiences I create work even after my subscription is over?

Not really. To keep the VR experiences working, it requires an active subscription.

Do you provide training for creating the experiences?

Well, the product has been designed in a way that ensures you don’t require training anytime. There are resources available on the website that can help you self-explore the product. And of course, if you have any questions, we are always just an email away!

What if I would like to have a training workshop for my team?

No worries. Get in touch with us at care@experizer.com and we will work out the details for you

I need to create a very specific experience and I am unable to find that in the library. How do I create it?

Our professional services team will be happy to custom develop the experience for you. For more details, get in touch with us at care@experizer.com

I have some support-related questions. Is there an email address I can reach you at?

Sure, get in touch with us at care@experizer.com

Can I create experiences on iPad

No. We don’t support experience creation on iPad at this moment.

What kind of usage-related reports does Experizer offer?

You can get multiple reports based on your Patron and Published Experience usage such as

  • Who has viewed which experience and for how many times
  • Which parts of the experience is viewed
  • How much time the viewers have spent on the experience.
How are the “Views” calculated?

Experizer considers a view when a viewer views the experience by clicking on the Experience Start button
